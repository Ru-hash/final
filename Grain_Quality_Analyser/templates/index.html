Grain Quality Analyser

NAM (National Agriculture Market) is an online portal introduced by the government, to connect all the farmers online with the traders, so that farmers get the best price for their produce. Our project aims to facilitate the process by introducing automatic grain quality assessment from an image of spread out sample of grain. This is not a well explored problem so we do everything from scratch, in very constrained conditions. We segment out each particle from the image of spread out wheat grain sample and classify it as grain or impurity. Our model is able to distinguish between grain and impurities with a validation accuracy of 90%.
